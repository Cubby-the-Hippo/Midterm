Types of Skis

This webpage serves to explain the different types of skis and which type you should choose based on your skiing preferences and needs.

Why Choosing the Right Type of Skis Matters

Choosing the correct type of skis that matches your skiing style and terrain is essential to improving your performance and enjoying your experience on the slopes.

Park Skis

Park skis are designed for freestyle skiing, ideal for performing tricks, jumps, and maneuvers in the terrain park.

Powder Skis

Powder skis are wider and designed for deep snow, giving better floatation and control in off-piste or backcountry skiing.

All-Mountain Skis

All-mountain skis are versatile and perform well on a variety of terrains, from groomed runs to variable snow conditions.
